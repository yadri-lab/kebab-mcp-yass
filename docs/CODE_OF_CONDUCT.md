# Code of Conduct

Kebab MCP follows the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][cc].

By participating in this project (issues, pull requests, discussions, or any
other interaction), you agree to uphold the standards described there.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## In short

- Be respectful and welcoming to all contributors regardless of background or
  experience level.
- Assume good faith. Disagree on ideas, not on people.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or sustained disruption.
- Keep discussions focused on improving Kebab MCP — this is a tool, not a forum
  for unrelated debates.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience behavior that violates the Covenant, please
report it privately to **citizenyass@gmail.com**.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The
maintainer is committed to keeping reporter identity confidential and will
not retaliate against good-faith reports.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may take any action they deem appropriate in response to
violations, including:

- A private warning
- A temporary ban from issues, PRs, or discussions
- A permanent ban for repeated or severe violations

Decisions are at the maintainer's discretion and final.
